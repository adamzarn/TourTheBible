<<A Psalm of thanksgiving.>> Shout for joy to Yahweh, all you lands! 100:2 Serve Yahweh with gladness. Come before his presence with singing. 100:3 Know that Yahweh, he is God. It is he who has made us, and we are his. We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture. 100:4 Enter into his gates with thanksgiving, into his courts with praise. Give thanks to him, and bless his name. 100:5 For Yahweh is good. His loving kindness endures forever, his faithfulness to all generations.
